About Us

__________________

TERRA Wolves Racing isn't just a club. We don't just build RC cars and dream of the impossible and call it a day. Ten80 means so much more to us than that. Sure, we're driven by the rush of building, designing, and competing with custom RC cars, but what keeps us coming back is the environment we've developed. Nothing prepares you better for the real world than developing close working relationships that foster immense amounts of creativity, expression, and innovation, and that's exactly what makes us love doing it so much: being able to call our fellow engineers not just friends, or teammates, or even roommates at competitions, but family. That's what it's all about.
